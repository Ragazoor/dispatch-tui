## Improving the Knowledge Base

The KB improves over time. Agents upvote entries that helped them
(higher upvote = higher rank) and downvote ones that misled them
("wrong" lowers the score, no human approval needed). Stale, low-scored
entries are archived automatically. From the KB overlay you can still
**x** (reject), **A** (archive), or **e** (edit) an entry to curate it.
