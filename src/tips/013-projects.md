## Projects Panel

Press **h/←** from the Backlog column (or **q** from anywhere on the board)
to open the Projects panel. Use **j/k** to navigate — the active project
switches instantly as you move. Press **l/→** or **Enter** to confirm and
return to the board.

Inside the panel: **n** creates a new project, **r** renames the selected
one, **d** deletes it (not allowed on the Default project), and **J/K**
reorders projects up or down.

The **Default** project is a catch-all: it shows tasks from every project,
making it a useful "all tasks" view regardless of which project they belong to.
